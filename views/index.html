>about

"touch is ten times stronger than verbal or emotional contact, and it affects damn near everything we do. no other sense can arouse you like touch. we forget that touch is not only basic to our species, but the key to it."

- Ashley Montagu

touch is incredibly important in social relationships. now, through technology we are able to see and hear each other even when we are apart. but where there is a webcam for seeing, and a microphone and speaker for hearing, there is not yet a dedicated technology to facilitate touch.

‘touch’ is a living structure formed of a networked audience’s connections. users’ hands populate the space and interact with each other digitally: dancing, playing, touching.

created by jessie zhang using three.js, handtrack.js and socket.io
